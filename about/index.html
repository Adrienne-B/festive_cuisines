About: Michael Dean

Born in Austin, Texas, Michael Dean has impacted all parts of the United States; but specifically the southern states. Since he is well-known for his masterpieces in BBQ and grilling. At 23 he graduated with his Bachelor's Degree in The International Culinary Schools at Art Institutes in Dallas. By the age of 32 Michael Dean has open over 150restaurants acrossed the nation.
